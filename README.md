This was our final project for BIS 205 at Eastern Ct State University Spring 2026
My project is an infromation system to keep track of all inventory in a coffee shop. In order to stay on top of it, organized, and ready for each day.
It is made on Excel
It was designed for managers, and employess, and baristias in the coffee shop.
it will help any business questions related to which item is the most popular/ sells out first in order to stay on track and not over order or under order inventory for the days. 
It will track the amount of inventory left in stock. what you will have to order for the day. It also tracks sales transactions. # BIS205
